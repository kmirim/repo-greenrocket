Functional Products. Targeted Applications.

We offer a full spectrum of pharmaceutical-grade cannabis materials — each designed for different stages of the value chain. From cultivation to processing, all products are handled under standardized, documented protocols.

Strains
(Flower Buds)

Standardized, organic biomass for extraction, pharmaceutical processing, or advanced formulation. Supplied in large volumes with full batch traceability.

Grown under natural sunlight in Stellenbosch, our biomass benefits from the region's Mediterranean climate — with long daylight hours, moderate temperatures, and fertile soils that enhance cannabinoid development. Supplied by weight and tailored to large-scale demands, this product is ideal for extraction labs and pharmaceutical manufacturers seeking consistent, compliant raw material.

Small Flower & Cream
(Trimmings and extract-grade Biomass)

Hand-trimmed, slow-cured cannabis flower grown in controlled indoor environments — ideal for pharmaceutical applications and clinical R&D.

Each flower is cultivated with precision in fully controlled conditions, allowing for consistent cannabinoid and terpene profiles across batches. After harvest, flowers are slow-cured and hand-trimmed to preserve their full potency, structure, and aroma. This is a premium-grade product, meeting the quality expectations of medical research and advanced extraction workflows.

K25
(Medicinal Use – Under Evaluation)

Rooted clones and tissue-cultured starter plants from verified mother stock, produced under strict protocols and available for licensed propagation or scientific research.

Our propagation program ensures that every plant originates from clean, stable genetics using tissue culture techniques to eliminate pathogens and preserve strain integrity. The result is uniform, predictable plant performance — ideal for licensed cultivators and institutions conducting scientific or commercial cannabis research.

Clones
(Nursery-Ready Plantlets)

A proprietary Green Rocket cultivar under ongoing clinical observation (e.g., Parkinson's symptom management). Available exclusively through pharmaceutical distribution.

K25, formerly known as Blood Bath, is in the process of formal registration under the Plant Improvement Act and has shown promising early results for medical application — including rapid symptomatic relief in neurological conditions. Production is limited and tailored to pharmaceutical or research institutions under strict compliance and documentation protocols.

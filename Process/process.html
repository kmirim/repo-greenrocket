Step-by-Step: The Green Rocket Process

Step by step

Our process is divided into 6 steps

01

Genetic selection & tissue culture

We use tissue culture and cloning techniques to ensure genetic stability, preserve cannabinoid profiles, and eliminate plant pathogens.

02

Mother stock & nursery phase

All strains begin in our controlled nursery — managed under pharma-grade standards and seasonal planning.

03

Climate-responsive cultivation

Grown in Stellenbosch, our plants benefit from natural sunlight and adaptive seasonal protocols, including autoflowering strategies for winter months.

04

Harvesting & drying

Harvest timing is strain-specific and optimized for medical use. Drying rooms are calibrated for moisture, temperature, and airflow.

05

Trimming & curing

Standardized curing periods and trimming ensure cannabinoid preservation and product consistency.

06

Testing, packaging & compliance

Every batch is tested for potency, purity and contaminants. Products are packaged under GACP/GMP-compliant protocols and traceable by batch.

Why It matters

Compliance is built into every step.

Green Rocket's production model is not just optimized — it's regulated. We follow Good Agricultural and Collection Practices (GACP), align with EU-GMP expectations, and operate under South Africa's Plant Improvement Act and Section 21/22 licensing. Every process is tracked, documented, and validated — from stem cell to storage.
